Sports Massage Therapy

ABOUT

My name is Ryan Ward and I am a fully qualified ITEC level 3 sports massage therapist. My treatments begin with a full postural assessment to assess any possible postural deformities.

After the assessment I then get you to carry out various different ranges of motion to see any muscles that are tight or fatigued. Once this is completed the massage begins. The massage usually lasts around 1 hour.

After the massage I will go over some home care advice and stretches to be carried out at home to improve your overall flexibility and lessen the strain on your body.
